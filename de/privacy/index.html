2. Overview

Exercise Snacks is an iOS mobile application for self-reflection and challenge management.

The App:

  • Does not require account registration or user account creation
  • Does not collect direct personal identifiers
  • Stores personal progress data locally on the device only

3. Information Collected

3.1 Information Stored Locally

The following data is stored exclusively on the user's device:

  • Check-ins and events
  • Progress values
  • Streak data
  • Badges and metrics
  • Settings
  • Internal local identifier ("user_1")

This data is not transmitted to the operator.
Legal Basis (GDPR): Article 6(1)(f) – Performance of a contract.

3.2 Anonymous Aggregated Statistics

To display anonymized community values, the App transmits aggregated metrics to Google Firebase (Firestore), including:

  • Integrity Score
  • Streak days
  • Level
  • Aggregated gameplay metrics

These statistics:

  • Are not linked to names or email addresses
  • Do not include user-generated text
  • Are associated only with a non-personal internal identifier

Regarding IP addresses:
The App does not intentionally transmit IP addresses. However, technical processing by Firebase infrastructure may occur as part of the connection process.

Provider: Google LLC (United States)
International Transfer: USA – based on Standard Contractual Clauses pursuant to Art. 46 GDPR.
Legal Basis (GDPR): Article 6(1)(f) – Legitimate interest in providing anonymous community-based statistics.

3.3 Payments and Subscription Processing

Subscriptions are processed exclusively through Apple Inc. via Apple In-App Purchases. RevenueCat is used for technical subscription management. RevenueCat receives an anonymous buyer identifier from Apple for this purpose.

The App does not collect payment card data.
Legal Basis (GDPR): Article 6(1)(b)

3.4 Push Notifications

The App may use Apple Push Notification Service (APNs) for functional notifications (e.g., reminders). No notification content is stored externally.

4. International Data Transfers

Data processed via Firebase, RevenueCat, and Apple may be transferred to the United States. Where required under the GDPR, such transfers rely on:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) pursuant to Art. 44 ff. GDPR
  • Additional safeguards where applicable

5. Data Retention

  • Local data: Stored until active deletion by the user, app reset, or app removal.
  • Anonymous external statistics: Retained for as long as the community feature is operated.

If you are located in the European Union or EEA, you have the right to:

  • Access

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority in your country of residence.

To submit a request, contact: Exercise Snacksdev@posteo.com

8. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided information, please contact us at Exercise Snacksdev@posteo.com.

9. Limitation of Liability for Data Loss

Since all personal data is stored exclusively on the user's device, no claim exists for restoration of lost data following a device change, reset, or app removal.
